# Brand Content Map

*Compiled 2026-07-13. Maps every major section of Carrie Davidson's official BrandStory (Soul Story Creative, July 2026) to a publication category, so anyone editing this PUBLIC repo knows what is safe to ship, what needs a pass, what stays internal, and what is still waiting on Carrie or on a source. No em-dashes anywhere, per voice canon.*

## The five categories

1. **Public website copy.** Ships as-is (or already live). Voice-compliant, no private data, no unverified claims.
2. **Public copy requiring light editing.** Public in substance, but needs de-personalizing, tone alignment, banned-word sweep, or figure removal before it goes on a page.
3. **Internal strategy only.** Never appears in the repo beyond what is already needed to build the site. Persona names, pricing, revenue math, do/don't marketing lists, launch strategy, agency contact.
4. **Requires Carrie's approval.** Public-facing in principle, but a person (Carrie) must confirm before it is published. Every item carries a `TODO(CARRIE)`.
5. **Requires factual or research verification.** Contains a claim that must be sourced or softened before publication. Every item carries a `TODO(CARRIE)` and cross-references RESEARCH-VERIFICATION.md.

Standing rule referenced throughout: never say the Method is "backed by" or "science-backed" by any study unless that study evaluated the Method itself. Preferred wording: "The method is informed by research on nervous-system regulation, trauma, and creative arts interventions."
