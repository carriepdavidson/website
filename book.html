Who this book is for

You have read the memoirs. You know the science.

Questions, answered plainly

About the book

What is Addicted to Trauma about?

Addicted to Trauma is my memoir about trauma and addiction as one wound telling itself in two languages. It is the story of how I lost myself one small leaving at a time, became the patient in the white hallway, and found my way back. It releases August 8, 2026.

When does the book release?

Addicted to Trauma releases August 8, 2026. Pre-ordering is the single most helpful thing a reader can do for a first book. Joining the waitlist gets you the release news, the pre-order link the day it exists, and the full first chapter.

Is it about addiction or trauma?

Both, because they are not separate problems. Trauma comes first and addiction is its result: the same wound telling itself in two different languages. The book traces that loop from the inside, without asking you to choose which story counts.

Where can I buy Addicted to Trauma?

Pre-order links go live on this page the moment they arrive from my publisher. Until then, join the waitlist and I will send you the link the day it exists. The memoir will be available in print and ebook.
