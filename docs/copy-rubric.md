# Copy Evaluation Rubric — Problem/Solution Fit (target ≥95/100)
*Used by the Fable evaluation loop for every COPY-PASS block. Scored per page-role, then site-weighted.*

| Criterion | Weight | What earns full marks |
|---|---|---|
| Problem recognition | 25 | Names the target customer's felt problem in HER OWN search language ("I understand it and still can't stop", loops, survival mode, addicted to stress/chaos) within the first 2 sentences. Reader thinks "she's describing me." |
| Solution clarity | 25 | What Carrie offers and the mechanism (nervous-system evidence, the 5-step Method, coaching container) is concrete — no vague transformation-speak. Reader knows exactly what happens next. |
| Differentiation | 20 | RN + lived experience + unified trauma/addiction thesis + named Method present and load-bearing, not decorative. |
| Voice fidelity | 20 | Intimate, clinically grounded, redemptive. Signature lines verbatim where used. Zero hype, scarcity theater, toxic positivity, shame-urgency. Two-layer rule intact (search-language headline + poetic payoff). |
| Action clarity | 10 | One obvious primary next step; secondary step is a gentle fallback (newsletter), not a competing door. |

**Kill criteria (auto-fail regardless of score):** loses a canon signature line's meaning; sounds like generic wellness marketing; overclaims clinical outcomes; breaks the search-language H1 layer.
