# Carrie Davidson — Brand Brief
*Compiled 2026-07-11 from carriepdavidson.com, warm-embrace-coaching.lovable.app, and Kajabi (Conscious Coaching Collective PLLC). Source of truth for the site rebuild.*

## Identity
- **Who:** Carrie Davidson, BSPH, BSN, RN — registered nurse + certified trauma recovery coach.
- **Positioning hook:** "The Nurse Who Became Her Own Patient." Lived experience + clinical training in complex trauma and addiction.
- **Business entity:** Conscious Coaching Collective, PLLC (support: coaching@carriepdavidson.com)
- **Book:** *Addicted to Trauma* — memoir, releasing Summer 2026 (currently in waitlist/pre-launch phase).

## The Book ↔ Coaching Overlap (the strategic core)
The memoir and the coaching are the **same thesis in two formats**:
> "Trauma and addiction are not separate problems. They are the same wound, telling itself in two different languages."

- The **book** is the story (proof of lived experience, emotional resonance, top-of-funnel).
- The **coaching** is the application (the method that got her out, offered to others).
- The **Method** is the bridge: **Witness → Understand → Interrupt → Create → Sustain** ("The Conscious Creation Method™" — grouped as Awareness / Regulation / Creation).
- Funnel shape: Book waitlist + free resources → Sunday Letter newsletter → quiz/workbook → coaching application.

## Voice (must never be lost)
- Intimate, clinically grounded, redemptive. Companionship over expertise-distance ("walking home," "slowly coming back to yourself").
- Short declarative headlines: "The loop runs faster than thought."
- Neuroscience precision + poetic reframe. Signature lines:
  - "You do not have to think your way out. You have to feel your way home."
  - "The loop is not a character flaw. It is a nervous system doing exactly what it was trained to do — until you give it new evidence."
  - "You are not broken. You are patterned. And patterns can change."
  - "Finally feel at home in your body."
  - "Insight-rich and transformation-poor" (names the target's exact frustration).
  - "You do not have to talk about it one more time to heal it."
- Never: hype, hustle-marketing, toxic positivity, clinical coldness, shame-based urgency.

## Target Audience
Primary: **high-achieving women** exhausted by trauma patterns — often therapy veterans ("tried therapy, apps, breathwork, workshops") who understand their patterns but can't break them. Adjacent: CPTSD, trauma-addiction overlap, ADHD/nervous-system dysregulation, addiction recovery.

## Current Site Weaknesses (fix in rebuild)
1. **Fragmented infrastructure:** carriepdavidson.com main site, but /coaching 307-redirects to `warm-embrace-coaching.lovable.app` (off-brand domain), and Kajabi lives at `consciouscoachingpllc.mykajabi.com`. Three surfaces, inconsistent naming ("Warm Embrace" vs "Conscious Coaching Collective" vs personal brand).
2. **Vague coaching offer:** by-application mystery, no methodology depth, duration, or investment signal on the main site.
3. **Too many equal-weight doors in** (memoir / workbook / coaching / quiz / newsletter) with no prioritized visitor pathway.
4. **Statistics without bridge:** "The Reality" stats section doesn't connect to how her method addresses them.
5. **Method stays abstract** until the workbook download.
6. **Book is 6+ months out** — anticipation without a conversion path in the meantime.

## Rebuild Principles
- One coherent brand surface under carriepdavidson.com; consistent naming.
- Clear primary pathway per visitor intent: *read the story* (book) / *fix the pattern* (coaching) / *start free* (workbook/quiz).
- Method made concrete on-page; book and coaching cross-sell each other explicitly.
- Voice preserved verbatim where it's already strong (signature lines above).
