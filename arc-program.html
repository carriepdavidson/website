Questions about the program

Is this therapy?

No. I’m a registered nurse and certified trauma recovery coach, not a therapist. Coaching works present-and-forward on patterns, regulation, and building what comes next. It can walk beside therapy without replacing it, and for many people, it works alongside their existing care.

How much time will it take each week?

One 60-minute session per week, plus small, doable between-session practices, nervous-system work that fits into a real life. No homework guilt. The practices are the point, but they’re gentle by design.

What if I’m not sure it’s the right fit?

That’s exactly what the discovery call is for. It’s a free conversation, not a pitch. We find the honest fit together, and if the ARC Program isn’t it, I’ll point you somewhere that is, even if it isn’t me.

Are payment plans available?

Yes. The program is $3,000, and payment plans are available. If cost is the sticking point, say so on the call and we will look at the options honestly, including the free places to begin.
