Common questions

What people ask before they begin

What is trauma recovery coaching?

Trauma recovery coaching is present-focused work on the patterns trauma leaves behind: seeing the loop, building the pause, and creating what comes next. Carrie Davidson is a registered nurse and certified trauma recovery coach. Coaching complements therapy. It does not replace it.

Why do the same patterns keep repeating even after years of insight?

Because the loop lives in the body as much as the mind. Insight alone does not always interrupt the pattern. What looks like a character flaw is a nervous system doing exactly what it was trained to do, until it is given new evidence.

What is the Conscious Creation Method?

The Conscious Creation Method is Carrie Davidson’s framework for breaking the trauma loop through nervous system regulation and creative healing. Three stages, Awareness, Regulation, Creation, opening into five movements: Witness, Understand, Interrupt, Create, and Build the intentional life.

How do you start?

Start free: the three-minute pattern quiz or the Sunday Letter. When you are ready for support, book a discovery call. It is a free half hour, just to talk, and there is no clock on the deeper work.

If you are in crisis, reach out now

You deserve immediate support. In the U.S.: call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) · text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line) · SAMHSA National Helpline 1-800-662-4357. If you are in immediate danger, call 911.
