About✦

The nurse who became her own patient.

I learned to read a room for danger before I learned to read a book. That vigilance made me an exceptional nurse. It also nearly cost me my life. Then came the day I became the patient in the white hallway who could not remember her own name.

I have been the nurse reading the chart and the patient inside the chart.

Both sides of it are what I bring to this work.

Common questions

What people ask about Carrie

Is Carrie Davidson a therapist?

No. Carrie Davidson is a registered nurse and certified trauma recovery coach. Coaching is present-and-forward work on patterns, regulation, and creation. It is not therapy, diagnosis, or medical treatment, and it can walk beside care from a licensed professional without replacing it.

What does trauma experienced mean?

Every practitioner in this space claims to be trauma-informed. Carrie Davidson is trauma experienced. She did not study trauma and apply frameworks to other people. She ran the patterns through her own body, came out the other side, and built a method from what actually worked.

What is the Conscious Creation Method?

The Conscious Creation Method is Carrie Davidson’s framework for breaking the trauma loop through nervous system regulation and creative healing. Three stages: Awareness, Regulation, Creation. Three stages opening into five movements: Witness, Understand, Interrupt, Create, and Build the intentional life.

How do you start working with Carrie Davidson?

Start with a free discovery call. It is a conversation, not a pitch. You talk first, Carrie listens and reflects back the pattern she hears. If the ARC Program is a fit, she says so plainly. The self-paced option is the Rooted and Rising course.
