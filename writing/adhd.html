Is it ADHD, or is it trauma?

When the wiring and the wound look the same.

Honest writing about the nervous system, recovery, and building a life that is actually yours.

Restlessness, distraction, overwhelm, a mind that will not settle: these can be signs of a different brain, or a body still braced from a different time, or both at once. This guide is for you if you have wondered which one you are living with, and felt how easily the two get mistaken for each other. Only a qualified clinician can assess or diagnose. What these essays offer is a clearer way to hold the question, without shame, so you can ask a better one.
