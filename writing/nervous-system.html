How to regulate your nervous system after trauma

You do not have to think your way out. You have to feel your way home.

Honest writing about the nervous system, recovery, and building a life that is actually yours.

Nervous system regulation is having a moment, and most of what gets said about it skips the part that matters here: what regulation looks like when your body learned, a long time ago, that calm was the thing that came right before danger. This guide is for you if you have tried the breathwork and the apps and still feel wired, braced, or suspicious of your own peace. The essays below work the problem from the body up, with neuroscience precision and none of the hype.
