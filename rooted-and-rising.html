A 6-week conscious creation journey · self-paced

Rooted & Rising

Build the Intentional Life. At Your Own Pace.

You cannot create a new life from a nervous system that is still bracing for the old one.

Most manifestation teaching starts with the wishlist. This course starts with the woman doing the wishing.

If you have tried the vision boards and the affirmations and quietly concluded that you are the one they don’t work for: you were never the problem. The method was. Affirmations bounce off a body that is braced for impact.

Rooted & Rising is conscious creation on a trauma-aware foundation, resting on the same Conscious Creation Method as my 1:1 coaching. Six weeks. Self-paced.

You don’t attract the life you want. You grow the capacity to receive it, and then you act like the woman who already lives there.

Begin with Ground

$222, one-time. The whole price, stated plainly. No countdown attached to it.

The shape of it

Six weeks, six phases, in an order that matters

  • 1 · Ground

    Safety first. Your nervous system as the instrument of creation, the three states to recognize in real time, and a daily anchor that actually settles you.

  • 2 · Clear

    Excavate the inherited beliefs running your life, and rewrite them with compassion instead of force.

  • 3 · Vision

    Permission to want. A written vision of your next chapter that feels true in your body, not just pretty on paper.

  • 4 · Embody

    Become her now: identity work, feeling-state rehearsal, and small environment edits that make her the path of least resistance.

  • 5 · Act

    Three brave, aligned moves, and the strangely difficult art of actually receiving.

  • 6 · Trust

    The gap, the wobble, and a practice you keep for life.

We ground before we clear, because belief work done in survival mode just hurts. We clear before we vision, because you can’t hear what you truly want over the noise of what you were told to want. And we act before we trust, because surrender only means something after you’ve done your part.

What’s included

What you get, and what to honestly expect

  • Six weekly modules, one unlocked each week to preserve the arc, readable in minutes and practiced across the week
  • One signature practice each week, done once, deeply
  • A daily 10 to 15 minute anchor ritual that evolves week to week
  • An affirmation each week that you body-test and adapt until it lands as true
  • The complete Rooted & Rising workbook: journal prompts, trackers, and reflection pages

By the end of six weeks you will have a daily practice that regulates your nervous system, the names of the three beliefs that have been running your life and a repeatable process for rewriting them, a written vision that lives in your body, at least three brave actions already taken, and more trust in yourself than you arrived with. That last one is the real product.

What this course is not: no hustle, no scarcity, no countdown timers, no promise that the universe is a vending machine. And it is education, not therapy: it does not diagnose or treat anything, and it walks beside any care you receive from licensed professionals rather than replacing it.

Is this you?

Who this is for

Women who are new to conscious creation: curious, maybe a little skeptical, and tired of hustle culture. People who tried affirmations before and felt like it didn’t work (it didn’t fail; it skipped the foundation). Women carrying old stories, from family, church, diet culture, or past relationships, about what they’re allowed to want. Anyone standing at a threshold: career change, post-divorce, empty nest, new decade, new chapter.

And if you’re drawn to deeper 1:1 trauma recovery work but not ready for that investment yet, this is the right first door.

I’m Carrie Davidson: registered nurse, certified trauma recovery coach, creator of the Conscious Creation Method™, and author of the memoir Addicted to Trauma. I built this course the way I build everything: from both sides of the chart.
