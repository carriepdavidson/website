Questions about the course

How is this different from the ARC Program?

Same Method, different container. Rooted & Rising is self-paced and self-guided, you move through it alone, on your schedule, for $222. The ARC Program is twelve weeks of private 1:1 coaching with me, for $3,000. Many people start here and go deeper later.

How long do I have access?

Lifetime access. The course is yours to keep and return to whenever the pattern visits again, because healing isn’t linear, and neither is this.

How much time does each week take?

An hour or two, spread however you like. You can move faster or slower than a week per module, nothing expires.

Is this therapy?

No. This is trauma recovery coaching and education, not therapy, diagnosis, or medical treatment. It can walk beside care from a licensed professional. It doesn’t replace it.
