# Content Roadmap: carriepdavidson.com

*Compiled 2026-07-12. For Carrie Davidson, BSPH, BSN, RN. A production backlog of ~32 article concepts grouped by search intent, built only on themes already present in her 12 published essays, the brand brief, the SEO/AEO research, and the live site. Voice canon applies to every future draft: search-language title, poetic payoff beneath, neuroscience precision, no fear-based or diagnostic framing, no em-dashes. Coaching is not therapy; education is not medical care.*

**How to read each entry.** Title / Reader question / Search intent / Topic cluster / Related resource / Next step / Review needed.

**Topic clusters in use:** cptsd, nervous-system, trauma-patterns, addiction-recovery, adhd-neurodivergence, nursing-hypervigilance, creativity-healing, personal-essays, the-memoir.

**Standing rules for every draft in this roadmap:**
- Lead the H1 and title tag with the literal phrase a tired woman would type at 11pm. Land Carrie's poetic line as the subhead or close (per research-seo.md two-layer pattern).
- Answer-first: the first 40 to 60 words after each header directly answer that header (per research-aeo.md).
- Named credentialed byline on every piece: "Carrie Davidson, BSPH, BSN, RN." Visible "Last updated" date.
- Never diagnose, never promise a cure, never use fear or shame urgency. Reframe, do not pathologize.
- No fabricated events, stats, credentials, or private life details. Any statistic must carry a linked primary source.
