ADHD Affirmation Cards

Gentle reframes for the days executive function feels out of reach.

Printable cards with small, kind reminders for the ADHD brain. Not toxic positivity, and not one more thing to be good at: just a few honest reframes to keep within reach on the days that ask a lot of you.

What it is

A printable set of affirmation cards written for the ADHD brain. Each card carries one gentle reframe for the moments when executive function stalls and the old, harsh self-talk gets loud.

Who it may help

It may help you if you have an ADHD brain and want small, kind reminders you can actually reach for on the hard days. Nothing here is a diagnosis or a fix; it is a little companionship in your own handwriting-sized format.

What is included

A printable PDF of affirmation cards you can print at home, cut out, and keep wherever the reminder helps: by your desk, in your bag, on the fridge. Keep the ones that land and set the rest aside.
