You’re Not Broken: Understanding the ADHD Brain

Your brain is not a broken version of a normal one. It is a different, workable design.

A 27-page ebook on why your brain works the way it does: executive function, rejection sensitivity, and practical tools that work with your wiring instead of against it. Less trying harder, more understanding what you are actually working with.

Download the ebook (free) →

What it is

A short, readable ebook that explains the ADHD brain from the inside: how executive function actually works, why rejection sensitivity hits so hard, and what changes when you stop treating your wiring as a flaw to fix and start treating it as a design to work with.

Who it may help

It may help you if you have an ADHD brain, diagnosed or wondering, or if you love someone who does, and you are tired of being told to just try harder. Nothing here is a diagnosis; it is understanding, offered plainly.

What is included

A 27-page ebook you can read on any device or print, covering executive function and rejection sensitivity, with practical tools you can try the same day. Read it straight through or return to the parts that fit your week.
