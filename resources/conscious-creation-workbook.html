Free Workbook

The Conscious Creation Workbook. Yours, free.

A 40-page, self-paced companion built on the Conscious Creation Method. The framework I developed for myself, over years of therapy and many modalities, for moving from the loop to something new, slowly, on your own time, in the quiet of your own room.

Download the workbook (free) →

What’s in it

Inside the workbook

Five parts, twenty-three exercises, one arc. The same method you met on this site, Awareness, Regulation, Creation, opened into five movements:

  • Witness. Somatic check-ins, mapping your nervous system baseline, and your first creative flow discovery.

  • Understand. Tracing the loop back to where it started and drawing your first map.

  • Interrupt. The activation protocol and a personal regulation toolkit.

  • Create. The full creative flow discovery, conscious language, and a daily gratitude practice.

  • Build. The intentional life. The corrected map, boundaries, weekly reflection, and a letter to your future self.

No paywall. No email gate. Just the framework I built for myself, the long way, over years. Yours to take home and work through at your own pace.
