What’s in the free library?

A growing shelf of short, practical guides, plus the quiz and the Sunday Letter. Each title opens a page that explains what it is and who it may help.

  • The 3-Minute Pattern Quiz

    Three minutes to see your pattern’s shape. If you’re new, start here.

    Take the quiz

  • The Trauma Tree Workbook

    The free download at the heart of the Method. Map the roots (what happened), the trunk (the beliefs), and the branches (the patterns) as one system.

    What it is · Download PDF

  • The Conscious Creation Workbook

    The Method in workbook form: prompts for all five steps and for building the intentional life that comes after regulation, one honest choice at a time.

    What it is · Download PDF

  • The Sunday Letter

    One honest essay and one small practice, most Sundays.

    Get the Sunday Letter

  • The Monthly Gathering

    A free monthly online gathering: teaching, sharing, community. First gathering this September.

    A workbook for making peace with the alarm system that kept you alive: learn to read your own body, then work with it instead of against it.

    What it is · Download PDF

    Named for the way we lose ourselves: one small leaving at a time. A journal for noticing the exits, and choosing to stay.

If you are in crisis, reach out now

You deserve immediate support. In the U.S.: call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) · text HOME to 741741 (Crisis Text Line) · SAMHSA National Helpline 1-800-662-4357. If you are in immediate danger, call 911.

Coaching is companionship alongside care, not a replacement for it. This work is trauma recovery coaching and education. It is not therapy, diagnosis, or medical treatment, and it is not a substitute for care from a licensed mental-health or medical professional. If you are working with a therapist or physician, coaching can walk beside that care. It does not replace it.
